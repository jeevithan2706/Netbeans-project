ABOUT:

  Entertaining amusement park for kids near Kanyakumari. A theme park where you can spend 3-4 hours in 15-20 rides. It's mostly not so crowded and you don't have to wait for turns for the rides. The water rides are good, especially the Family and Kids pool. Water quality is good. The Wave Pool is small but enjoyable Baywatch Amusement park is really a great place for spending time with family/friends. A place to enjoy with children, family and friends. There is a wax museum attached to this Amusement park. Kids will definitely enjoy the water slides, wave pools. Also, there are rides like trains and bumper cars.
