HOTEL SRI DEVI

This property is 12 minutes walk from the beach. Facing the beachfront, Hotel Sridevi offers 3-star accommodation in Kanyakumari and features a bar, garden and terrace. With free WiFi, this 3-star hotel offers room service and an ATM. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests. The hotel offers a continental or à la carte breakfast. Nāgercoil is 16 km from Hotel Sridevi. The nearest airport is Thiruvananthapuram International, 100 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
